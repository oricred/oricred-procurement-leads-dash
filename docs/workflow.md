# Oricred Lead Generation and Transaction Funding Workflow

This workflow is the authorized pipeline for turning tender awards into funding opportunities.

## Source Flow

1. New Lead
2. Client Contacted
3. Qualified Lead or Lost Lead
4. Won Opportunity
5. Credit Preparation
6. Credit Review
7. Conditions Precedent / Pre-Approved
8. Term Sheet Sent
9. Term Sheet Received
10. Contracts Sent
11. Contracts Received
12. Ready to RFF
13. Funded

## Application Mapping

Awarded tenders become opportunities in `new_lead`. The awarded supplier is the lead company, the award value is the potential funding size, and the buyer/tender data supplies context for scoring and contact prioritization.

Assignments are metadata on an opportunity, not a pipeline stage. A user can own a card while it remains in the correct workflow column.

## Stage Definitions

| Stage | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| New Lead | Awarded company identified from a watched tender. |
| Client Contacted | Initial engagement and funding need assessment started. |
| Qualified Lead | Company appears eligible and worth pursuing. |
| Lost Lead | Lead is rejected, unresponsive, unsuitable, or not proceeding. |
| Won Opportunity | Client agrees to proceed with Oricred. |
| Credit Preparation | Documents and credit paper are being prepared. |
| Credit Review | Credit committee assessment is underway. |
| Conditions / Pre-Approved | Decision issued with conditions or pre-approval. |
| Term Sheet Sent | Commercial terms have been issued. |
| Term Sheet Received | Client has accepted or returned the term sheet. |
| Contracts Sent | Legal agreements have been issued. |
| Contracts Received | Executed agreements have been returned. |
| Ready to RFF | Final checks before Request for Funding. |
| Funded | Funds released. |
